# Accelerator Research: AngelPad, Entrepreneur First (EF), and Antler

**Research Date:** March 23, 2026
**Focus:** Selection criteria, application process, funding, acceptance rates, India/international founder considerations

## 1. ANGELPAD

### Overview
- **Type:** Seed-stage accelerator (NOT pre-idea; requires existing startup)
- **Location:** San Francisco and New York City
- **Founded:** 2010 by Thomas Korte (former Google product manager) and Carine Magescas
- **Duration:** 10-week intensive program
- **Frequency:** 2 cohorts per year (every 6 months)
- **Cohort Size:** ~15-20 teams per batch
- **Ranking:** Ranked #1 U.S. Accelerator by MIT every year since 2015
- **Track Record:** 150+ companies launched, 3 unicorns, 41 exits, $2.2B+ total raised by portfolio companies

### 1.1 Selection Criteria

**What they look for:**
- Exceptional founding TEAMS (team over idea)
- At least one technical developer/co-founder on the team
- "The immigrant spirit" -- willingness to adapt, hustle, and expand
- Strong technical capabilities and product development abilities
- Market growth potential in BIG markets tackling BIG problems
- Team execution capabilities and chemistry
- Early traction or MVP demonstration preferred

**Sectors accepted:**
- Industry agnostic across tech: SaaS, Marketplaces, Core Technology, Advertising, API, Mobile, Healthcare, AI, Data, B2B, B2C, Drones, and more
- Recently added specialized tracks in AI and enterprise SaaS (2025 curriculum update)
- No explicit crypto/web3 focus mentioned

**Solo founders:** Not explicitly addressed, but heavy emphasis on team dynamics and co-founder chemistry suggests teams are strongly preferred. Must have at least one technical co-founder.

**Revenue/MRR requirements:** No specific MRR threshold, but early traction or MVP is a positive signal.

### 1.2 Application Process

1. **Online Application:** Submit information about business and founding team
2. **Video Pitch:** Described as "90% of the success" -- this is critical
3. **Documentation:** Business plan, financial projections, pitch deck (under 15 slides recommended)
4. **Shortlist:** ~200 teams shortlisted for interviews from ~4,000 applications
5. **Interview:** 25-minute in-person interview with Thomas Korte and Carine Magescas
   - Focus areas: market understanding, team decision-making, growth potential, product vision
   - Co-founder dynamics are evaluated
6. **Decision:** Follow-up within 24 hours recommended

**Timeline:** Applications are currently CLOSED (as of March 2026). You can sign up at angelpad.com/more to be notified when the next cohort opens. Historically runs two batches per year.

### 1.3 Acceptance Rate

- **Less than 1%** -- extremely selective
- ~15-20 teams selected from ~4,000 applications per cycle
- One of the hardest accelerators to get into globally

### 1.4 Funding Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Investment | $120,000 per startup |
| Equity | 7% stake |
| Cloud Credits | $300,000+ in credits from AWS, Google, DigitalOcean |
| Program Fee | None mentioned |

### 1.5 International / Indian Founders

- "Anyone from anywhere can apply"
- Most companies are based in SF and NYC, but founders come from all over the world
- International founders from Canada, Australia, and other countries have been accepted
- **No India-specific program or presence**
- **No specific mentions of Indian founders in portfolio**
- Founders need to be present in SF or NYC for the 10-week program
- Visa/work authorization may be a challenge for international founders (not addressed by AngelPad)

### 1.6 Notable Portfolio Companies
- Postmates, Buffer, MoPub, Sensor Tower, Gatik ($700M+ valuation), CookUnity ($70M raised)

### 1.7 Key Links
- Website: https://angelpad.com/
- Apply / Get Notified: https://angelpad.com/more/
- About: https://angelpad.com/about/
- Portfolio: https://angelpad.com/portfolio/

## 2. ENTREPRENEUR FIRST (EF)

### Overview
- **Type:** Pre-team, pre-idea talent investor and company builder (NOT a traditional accelerator)
- **Active Locations (2026):** London, Bangalore, San Francisco
- **Founded:** 2011 in London by Matt Clifford and Alice Bentinck
- **Duration:** ~24 weeks total (12 weeks FORM + 12 weeks LAUNCH)
- **Cohort Size:** 50-60 individuals per cohort
- **Key differentiator:** You apply as an INDIVIDUAL, not a team. No idea needed.

### 2.1 Selection Criteria

**What they look for (4 key ingredients):**
1. **Ambition:** Innate, determined urge to build something globally important
2. **Edge (Unfair Advantage):** Deep technical knowledge, market experience, or obsessive first-hand expertise in a domain
3. **Outlier Trajectory:** Exceptional achievements relative to peers
4. **High Motivation:** Biggest red flag is lack of motivation

**Who should apply:**
- People in early career stages OR with a few years of industry experience
- Those who have achieved exceptional outcomes relative to their peers
- Technical AND non-technical founders (cohorts split roughly 50/50)
- People obsessed with big ideas, not "overly obvious" ones

**What you DON'T need:**
- No idea required
- No co-founder required (you find one during the program)
- No startup experience required
- No revenue/traction required

**Solo founders:** EXPLICITLY welcomed. The entire model is built around individuals who don't yet have co-founders.

**Pre-idea:** EXPLICITLY welcomed. Most applicants have "a range of areas they're interested in" but no specific idea.

### 2.2 Program Structure

**FORM Phase (12 weeks, in home city -- Bangalore or London):**
- Co-founder matching: Meet other talented individuals, pair up, test working together
- Idea generation and validation
- "Pairing up and breaking up" is normal and expected
- Investment Committee pitch at the end of FORM
- ~1/3 of participants get funded at this stage

**LAUNCH Phase (12 weeks, San Francisco):**
- Teams that pass Investment Committee relocate to SF
- Rapid product development and market validation
- Fundraising preparation
- Demo Day in SF
- Companies typically raise $1-7M within weeks of Demo Day

### 2.3 Application Process -- EF Bangalore

**Step-by-step:**
1. Online application (10-12 questions): name, email, LinkedIn, background
2. 30-minute get-to-know-you call
3. 15-minute technical assessment
4. 15-minute behavioral interview
5. Offer / rejection

**Current Timeline (Fall 2026 Bangalore Cohort):**
- Program starts: August 2026
- Application deadline for Round 2 consideration: May 1, 2026
- Three selection rounds planned
- Applications reviewed on rolling basis -- apply early

**Other cohorts:** London and SF cohorts also run; EF also launching "The Bridge" program in 2026 (3 cohorts planned).

### 2.4 Acceptance Rate

- **~3-10% acceptance rate** (varies by source and cohort)
- One source states 1.5% (2x as selective as claimed by EF)
- Each cohort: 50-60 individuals selected
- Of those accepted, ~1/3 end up with a funded company
- Of that 1/3, ~50% successfully close a seed round
- **Effective pipeline from application to funded company is extremely narrow**

### 2.5 Funding Details

| Stage | Amount | Terms |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Talent Investment (FORM) | INR 300,000 (~$3,500) | No equity, no repayment. For living costs during FORM. Gross of tax. |
| Main Investment | $125,000 | Post-money SAFE for 8% equity |
| Optional Additional (Transpose Platform) | $125,000 | Uncapped MFN SAFE (requires SF relocation + Delaware C-Corp) |
| **Total Potential** | **Up to $250,000** | **8% equity (main) + additional for optional** |

**Note:** Older cohorts reportedly had different terms ($55K for 10%), which some founders found steep. Current terms ($125K for 8%) are more founder-friendly.

### 2.6 India / Bangalore Specific Details

- **EF has a full Bangalore program** -- one of only 3 active locations globally
- FORM phase (first 12 weeks) takes place entirely in Bangalore office
- LAUNCH phase requires relocation to San Francisco
- INR 300,000 talent investment for living costs during FORM
- EF does NOT provide visas but can offer support with applications
- Indian founders are a core part of the program
- Founders from diverse Indian cities participate
- Contact: support@joinef.com

**Recent program changes (2026):**
- EF closed France and Germany programs
- Refocusing European operations to scout talent for UK and US programs
- Bangalore remains a core hub alongside London and SF

### 2.7 Patterns & Insights

- The co-founder matching process can be "overwhelming" -- expect to pair up and break up multiple times in 8 weeks
- Fellow cohort members and EiRs (Entrepreneurs in Residence) provide strong support
- Wide network access to mentors, investors, experienced founders
- The fast pace and high expectations create significant pressure
- Best co-founders "energize you and make you super productive"
- EF is "obsessed with big ideas and bored by anything overly obvious"
- Technical founders (ML/AI, engineering) are in high demand

## 3. ANTLER

### Overview
- **Type:** Pre-seed / Day Zero investor and residency program
- **Global Presence:** 27+ cities across 6 continents
- **India Presence:** Strong -- Bangalore-based residency program
- **Founded:** 2017 in Singapore
- **Program Format:** Residency (3-6 weeks for AI track; longer for general)
- **Key differentiator:** Accepts solo founders, pre-idea, AND early teams. Very strong India presence with AI focus.

### 3.1 Selection Criteria

**Three types of founders sought:**
1. **Commercial:** Leadership skills, experience launching products, sales expertise
2. **Technical:** Engineering, AI/ML, deep tech backgrounds
3. **Domain Experts:** Deep industry expertise in a specific vertical

**Three key qualities:**
1. Self-starters who set and achieve ambitious goals
2. Clear advantage/edge with deep expertise
3. Steadfast dedication and persistence

**Critical requirement:** Must commit FULL-TIME. Cannot work alongside a job, degree, another company, or freelance.

**Solo founders:** EXPLICITLY accepted. "Before Day Zero" community specifically for solo founders seeking co-founders.

**Pre-idea:** EXPLICITLY accepted. Program designed to support founders "from the very beginning, even before they have a fully formed team or business idea."

**Revenue/traction:** Not required. Accepts pre-launch, pre-product, pre-revenue teams.

**Sectors (Industry-agnostic but with AI emphasis):**
- AI/ML-native startups (strongest current focus, ~7 out of 11 recent companies in AI/deeptech)
- Consumer, Fintech, Enterprise, SaaS
- "Bharat" problems: mobility, farmer lending, SMB supply chain
- Healthcare, PropTech, Creator economy, EdTech
- ONDC-based e-commerce
- Deep tech

### 3.2 Application Process

**Step-by-step:**
1. **Online Application:** Submit background, motivation, expertise (rolling basis)
2. **Interview 1:** Structured interview with Antler team
3. **Interview 2:** Second structured interview
4. **Possible Interview 3 or Video Task:** Some candidates get a third interview
5. **Acceptance into Residency**
6. **Residency (in-person, Bangalore):** 3-6 weeks depending on track
7. **Investment Committee Pitch:** Present to IC for funding decision
8. **Investment Decision:** Within 3 weeks for AI Residency track

**Total interviews: 2-3 (sometimes plus video task)**

### 3.3 Application Timeline

**Antler India:**
- Applications are open on a rolling basis year-round
- AI Residency 2nd Edition deadline was February 19, 2026 (now passed)
- New cohorts launching quarterly (every ~45 days as of 2026 strategy shift)
- Next India cohort dates: Check https://www.antler.co/residency/india for updates
- Note: India was NOT listed on the global cohort start dates page for 2026 -- India operates its own schedule

**Other Antler locations (2026):**
- Indonesia (Jakarta): March 30, 2026
- UAE/Saudi (Dubai, Riyadh): April 6, 2026
- Kenya (Nairobi): April 13, 2026
- Japan (Tokyo): May 11, 2026
- Australia (Sydney/Melbourne/Brisbane): August 17, 2026
- UK (London): September 22, 2026

### 3.4 Acceptance Rate

- **1.4% for India's 4th cohort** (110+ founders from 9,200 applications / 7,700+ submissions)
- **~2% for UK (London)** -- described as "more competitive than Oxford or Harvard"
- **General range: 1.4-3%** depending on location and cohort
- **Post-residency funding rate: 20-45%** of accepted founders receive investment (higher for AI Residency: 50-75%)

### 3.5 Funding Details

| Track | Investment | Equity | Additional |
|-------|-----------|--------|------------|
| **Standard Residency** | $100K-$190K | 10-12% | Varies by location |
| **AI Residency (India)** | ₹4 Crore (~$470K) | ~11% | $1M-$3M in AI perks |
| **Global (xRaise data)** | $200K for 8% | 8% | Additional $300K follow-on possible |
| **Total Potential** | Up to $500K | -- | $30M follow-on fund available |

**Additional support:**
- ₹2 Lakh one-time relocation grant (India)
- Complimentary office space during residency
- $1M+ in founder perks (cloud credits, AI tools, etc.)
- EUR 2,000 one-time grant (some locations)

### 3.6 India / Indian Founders -- Deep Dive

**India Track Record:**
- 550+ individuals and early teams backed in India
- Portfolio of 80+ Indian startups
- $15M set aside specifically for India residency program
- Antler India is doubling check sizes in 2026, especially for AI founders

**4th Cohort Demographics (most recent large cohort):**
- 110+ founders selected (1.4% acceptance rate)
- Average 11 years work experience
- 61% previously in senior roles at unicorns or Fortune 500
- 50% are repeat entrepreneurs
- Founders from beyond metros: Kochi, Varanasi, Indore, Jaipur, Rohtak, Surat
- Diverse backgrounds: pilots, doctors, racing drivers, hackathon winners

**AI Focus in India (2026 strategy):**
- Antler India doubling check sizes for AI-native founders
- Compressed process to just a few weeks
- ~7/11 recent cohort companies in AI/deeptech
- "India missed out on chips and models but real opportunity is in applications"

**"Before Day Zero" Community:**
- Invite-only community for solo founders
- For people 3-6 months away from starting
- Co-founder matching
- No application cost

**Antler India Fellowship:**
- Separate program for freshers/early-career individuals
- Different from the main residency

### 3.7 Patterns & Insights

- Antler is shifting to smaller, more curated cohorts (fewer companies, more support)
- 45-day cycle with new cohort every quarter (2026 change)
- Heavy AI/deeptech tilt -- 40%+ of investments in AI as of 2024, likely higher now
- Strong "Bharat" thesis -- interested in India-specific problems, not just global SaaS
- Founders with 10+ years experience preferred over fresh graduates for main residency
- Repeat entrepreneurs make up ~50% of cohorts
- Full-time commitment is non-negotiable

## RECOMMENDATIONS FOR INTERNATIONAL/INDIAN FOUNDERS

### If you are a solo founder with no idea:
1. **EF Bangalore** -- Purpose-built for this. Apply for Fall 2026 by May 1, 2026.
2. **Antler India** -- Also accepts pre-idea solo founders. Check for next quarterly cohort.

### If you are building in AI:
1. **Antler India AI Residency** -- Largest checks (~₹4Cr), fastest process (3 weeks), heavy AI focus
2. **EF Bangalore** -- Strong technical founder pool, SF Demo Day access

### If you have a team + product/traction:
1. **AngelPad** -- Best brand, highest selectivity, but requires US presence and team
2. **Antler** -- Can still accept early teams with traction

### If you are based in India:
1. **Antler India** -- Strongest India presence (550+ individuals backed, 80+ startups)
2. **EF Bangalore** -- Full program in Bangalore, then SF for LAUNCH phase
3. **AngelPad** -- No India presence; would need to relocate to US
